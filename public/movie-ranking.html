The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Beauty and the Beast

I really love Beauty and the Beast because of it's visual splendor, storytelling, memorable characters and the way it touches upon fundamental aspects of human experience..

Robin-B-Hood

It's a very nice movie for me due to it's unique take of Jackie Chan's charismatic performance, the blend of action and humor, and it's ability to resonate emotionally with viewers.

White Chicks

I also loved this movie because of it's comedic approach, memorable performances, cultural impact, and the ability to entertain audiences with its over-the-top yet endearing story.
